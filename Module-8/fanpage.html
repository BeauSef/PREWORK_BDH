Bio

Motley Crue is an American heavy metal band from Los Angeles, Califronia.
The group was surely a wild bunch ever since they formed on January 17, 1981.
The band has sold more than 100 million records worldwide, and 25 million in the US.
Motley Crue has many hit songs inlcluding Girls, Girls, Girls, Shout at the Devil, and Kickstart my heart.
